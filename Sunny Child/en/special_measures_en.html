Children with special needs

The children from the register with special needs are those children who, for various reasons, have not found their Mom and Dad. These children are with various special needs, with family burdens, completely healthy physically and mentally, groups of brothers and sisters, or children who are over ten years of age. Every child is unique in their own way and has different needs, but they all need love, support and understanding.

Waiting Children Program

In order to increase the chance of these children to find a family, the Ministry of Justice has introduced a special procedure. Every month, the Ministry publishes on its website brief information about children for whom there are no registered adoptive parents. The Ministry of Justice provides detailed information and photographs of the specific child to the accredited organizations. The accredited organization has two months to find a suitable family.
